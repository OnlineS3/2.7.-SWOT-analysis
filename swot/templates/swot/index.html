About SWOT Analysis

SWOT Analysis is a simple process designed to help identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. The process aims to inform a competitive strategy perspective in terms of improving and creating sustainable growth and a positive societal impact.
Identifying issues relevant to each of the four quadrants of SWOT (strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats) will help identify promising areas of research and innovation. Furthermore, it will help identify areas that could be more deeply analysed.
We provide a tool that lets you record, organise and edit strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats online. You can also share your drafts of your SWOT analysis with others who can then contribute to the overall analysis.
