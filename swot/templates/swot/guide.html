Guide

Splash Page

Use the ‘Create Simple SWOT Analysis’ button to create a simple version of the SWOT analysis. The simple version is shorter and quicker to complete.

Use the ‘Create Advanced SWOT Analysis’ button to create an advanced version of the SWOT analysis. The advanced version is longer and offers more in depth analysis.

Use the ‘Load SWOT Analysis’ button to load a SWOT Analysis that has been saved to your account.

SWOT Menu

Use the ‘Save’ and ‘Load’ to save or load a SWOT Analysis to your account.

Use the ‘Upload from JSON’ and the ‘Export to JSON’ to save or load a SWOT Analysis in the form of a JSON file on your computer.

Use the ‘Export to PDF’ button to export your SWOT analysis to a report in the form of a PDF file.

Use the ‘Share’ button to access your SWOT analysis’ Share ID. Another user can use the Share ID to access your SWOT analysis when they load one.
