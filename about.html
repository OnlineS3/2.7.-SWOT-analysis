About SWOT Analysis

SWOT Analysis is a simple tool designed to help identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. This aims to inform a competitive strategy perspective in terms of improving and creating sustainable growth and a positive societal impact.
Identifying issues relevant to each of the four quadrants of SWOT (strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats) will help identify promising areas of research and innovation. Furthermore, this will help identify areas that could be more deeply analysed.
